Our Divisions

Specialized departments working in unison to engineer the perfect striker.

Player Selection Department

Responsible for scouting and identifying top high school talent across Japan. Utilizes advanced metrics to find players with the potential for "egoistic" evolution.

Psychological Development Unit

Focuses on the mental conditioning of candidates. Designs scenarios to test resilience, ego, and decision-making under extreme pressure.

Performance Science Lab

Analyzes physical data to optimize training regimens. Monitors nutrition, recovery, and biomechanics to ensure peak physical condition.

Tactical Analysis Division

Deconstructs modern football tactics to create training simulations. Provides real-time feedback on player positioning and spatial awareness.

Operations & Logistics

Manages the daily operations of the Blue Lock facility, including facility maintenance, security, and resource management.
