Who We Are

The Blue Lock Organization is a private sports-performance agency dedicated to creating the world's best striker.

Our Mission

To develop the ultimate ego-driven striker using science, analytics & psychological evolution. We believe that Japanese football needs a revolutionary change, and that change begins with a single, overwhelming ego.

Our methods are controversial, our training is rigorous, but our results are undeniable. We are not just building athletes; we are engineering the future of the sport.

Key Objectives

  • Identify and cultivate elite talent from across Japan.
  • Implement cutting-edge data analytics in player development.
  • Foster a "survival of the fittest" environment to awaken latent potential.
  • Win the World Cup.

History

Founded in 2018 following the Japan National Team's elimination from the World Cup, the Blue Lock project was initiated by the Japan Football Union (JFU) under the guidance of Anri Teieri.

Recognizing the lack of a decisive striker, the organization hired the enigmatic coach Jinpachi Ego to design a training regimen that would produce a revolutionary player.

Global Operations

Headquarters: Tokyo, Japan

Our state-of-the-art facility, known as "Blue Lock," is a massive, prison-like dormitory and training center designed to isolate players from the outside world, allowing them to focus entirely on their evolution.
