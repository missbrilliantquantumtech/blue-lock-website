The Blue Lock Program

A multi-stage evolution process designed to create the world's best striker.

01

Entry Trials

300 high school forwards are invited. Only those who pass the initial "Tag" game proceed. The weak are eliminated immediately.

02

Ranking & Ego Metrics

Players are ranked 1-300 based on their performance. Lower-ranked players live in poor conditions, while top players enjoy luxury. Rankings fluctuate daily.

03

Team Rotations

Candidates form teams (Team V, Z, etc.) and compete in a round-robin tournament. Only the top teams and top scorers survive.

04

Elite 5 Training

Survivors face off against world-class players to test their abilities on a global standard.

05

U-20 & Pro Selection

The final selection. The Blue Lock Eleven is formed to challenge the Japan U-20 team and secure their place as the future of Japanese football.
